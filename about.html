About

Hi, I'm Minaya — a global marketer, ex-entrepreneur, and relentlessly curious human.

I'm a high-performer with a soul. A digital marketer, global strategist, and ex-entrepreneur who's happiest helping impact-first businesses grow — and no, I don't mean the generic mission-vision stuff. I mean companies that actually give a crap about the world and the people in it.

I've spent 8+ years building a career in the startup ecosystem across three continents — from navigating the high-speed chaos of a Series E Silicon Valley startup in Berlin, to co-founding a yoga retreat venture in Spain, to scaling an eating disorder recovery platform in the US. Lately, I've been leading D2C lifecycle strategy, marketing ops, and AI adoption at a global mobility brand.

My toolkit lives at the intersection of growth strategy, CRM & lifecycle marketing, funnel optimization, workflow automation, and AI integration. But what really drives me? Impact. A team that's all in. Building something worth caring about.

I'm organized and tenacious (Capricorn energy duh), but I also lead with empathy and warmth. I love digging into data as much as I love understanding the human behind it.
